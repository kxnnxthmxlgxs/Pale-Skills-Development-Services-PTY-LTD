PALE SKILLS DEVELOPMENT SERVICES (PTY) LTD

Company Background


PALE operates through Agriculture and Rural Development. It is a B-BBE Level 1 with a 100% black ownership. We are a company dedicated to contribute to the growth of rural agricultural sector through transformation for communities .To meet our customer's needs in everything we do we pursue perfection. For communities at large we offer targeted training, take responsibilities of their environment and be part of the following initiatives:


Agriculture & Rural Development to communities, Producers, Farmers, Youth, Women’s League, Co-Operatives, NGO’s and all users of Agricultural products nationwide. Pale Skills Development Services (Pty)Ltd is an accredited training provider registered with AgriSeta.
